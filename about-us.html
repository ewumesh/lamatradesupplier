Mission & Vision

Lama Trade Supplier is one of the finest leading construction equipment and materials in town. It was established in 2016 A.D with an intention of providing construction materials in large scale and small-scale retail prices too. During its establishment there were very few companies providing such services to the people. Since, then we have been satisfying all kinds of customers needs and demands from the day of its establishment. Lama Trade Suppliers mainly engage in manufacturing and supplying construction materials like cement, Marbles, Pipes, lightweight building materials, glass fiber, fiber- reinforced plastic products and many more in small scale and large scale with suitable prices prevailing in town. The company is well known as one of the best construction supplier company recognized by the government of Nepal and highly accepted by customers. We have very professional and experienced employees who work very hard to fulfill customer needs and demands. We import international brand construction materials and also help in exporting Nepalese brand around the world.

We believe that "Quality is more important than quantity". Therefore, we are mainly focused on providing high quality materials to customers. Without standard construction materials we cannot build something that can last or withstand during natural disasters and can lead to collapse of infrastructures with loss of valuable human life. We assure that we are aware of all kinds issues and we have a great reputation of not receiving any kinds of customer complaints.
